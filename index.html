After spending over 11 years as an electrical and commissioning engineer — verifying complex systems, troubleshooting high-stakes technical infrastructures, and coordinating multi-million-dollar project deliveries — I made a deliberate pivot into the world of Technical Virtual Assistance and Automation. If there's a system to optimize, I want to be the one optimizing it.
